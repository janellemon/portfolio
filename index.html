About Me

I am 16 years old, and I'm from Oakland California. Some of my interests are musical theatre, and volunteering with kids. In my free time I like to watch Netflix and hang out with my friends. My favorite places to shop are Urban Outfitters, and Indigo Vintage Co-op in Berkeley. I enjoy hiking, running, and other activities in nature, and my favorite color is dark green.

Places I hope to visit in the future are Greece, Italy, Morocco, and Australia.
